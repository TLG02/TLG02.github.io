Touranments

A chess tournament is a series of chess games played competitively to determine a winning individual or team .


There are two typres of tournaments Ranked and Unranked.


In addition to this, chess tournaments follow the time controls of : Bullet, Blitz, Rapid and Classical.


Classical time controls are used mostly for over the board tournaments and these games can last for hours ,for example 2 hours with 40 mins being added once move 40 is reached.


Bullet refers to any match that is 1|0 or 2|1. It`s any match that is faster than three mins per player.


Rapid is anything that is longer than ten minus, time controls of Rapid are usually - 15 mins with 10 sec`s being added on for each move made, or 30|0 - this means 30 mins time control for each player.


Blitz time controls are 3|0, 3|2, 5|0, 5|5 .
